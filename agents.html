Valorant is a 5v5 first person shooter game featuring 19 unique agents.

Moyens I/O

Valorants different character roles are initiator, sentinel, duelist and controller. These are the different roles that all characters fit into.

You either defend or attack different sites in order to win the game. You win the game by winning 13 rounds and whoever attacks/deffends changes after 12 rounds. In order to win rounds the attackers have to either kill the entire enemy team or plant a spike that detonates after 40 seconds. As a deffender you have to either kill the entire enemy team or defuse the spike when it has been planted to win.

AGENTS

The game features 19 unique agents. All of which deserves a spot in one of the four roles. There are currently eighteen agents in the game: Astra, Breach, Brimstone, Chamber, Cypher, Jett, Kay/O, Killjoy, Neon, Omen, Phoenix, Raze, Reyna, Sage, Skye, Sova, Viper, and Yoru. (Mobalytics 2022)

all pictures by valorant wiki

Duelists are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first. This class type is the most offensive choice and often ranks highly as an Attacker. These agents will generally be at the forefront when clearing corners and attacking sites. (RANKEDBOOST 2022)

picture by valorant

Controllers are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success. This class provides lots of utility in the form of slowing down the enemy players via Walls, Smokes that block Vision, and even Slows that can be placed on the ground. They try to take controll of the map for their team. (RANKEDBOOST 2022)

picture by wikipedia

Initiators challenge angles by setting up their team to enter contested ground and push defenders away. You can expect to see lots of stuns and information gathering abilities with this class. The initiators try to set their team up for victory by clearing angles/corners and helping them winning gun-fights. (RANKEDBOOST 2022)

picture by wikipedia

Sentinels are defensive experts who can lock down areas and watch flanks, both on attacker and defender rounds. This class type tends to be the best defensive agents; these characters will either hang back or lay traps to watch your teams flank when attacking. On defense they anchor sites to kill opponents pushing. (RANKEDBOOST 2022)

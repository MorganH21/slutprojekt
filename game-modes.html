GAME-MODES

VALORANT features multiple game modes categorized as Plant/Defuse or Deathmatch type. There are permanent modes that include Competitive, Unrated, Spike Rush, and Deathmatch. There's also a lineup of rotating game modes that change every patch update (like Snowball Fight). (CODASHOP 2021)

in unrated, you either defend or attack different sites in order to win the game. You win the game by winning 13 rounds and whoever attacks/deffends changes after 12 rounds. In order to win rounds the attackers have to either kill the entire enemy team or plant a spike that detonates after 40 seconds. As a deffender you have to either kill the entire enemy team or defuse the spike when it has been planted to win. also my own take

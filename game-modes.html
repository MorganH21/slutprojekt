there are about 6 game-modes

unrated which is the main gamemode can be played unranked and ranked. spike rush is unrated but with some twists. deathmatch is a 12 player all out battle to get the most kills. replication is unrated but every team has 5 players of the same character. snowball fight is self-explanitory and a winter-only type game-mode. escalation is like gungame so you have to get an amount of kills to move on to the next gun, 5v5 as well.

in unrated, you either defend or attack different sites in order to win the game. You win the game by winning 13 rounds and whoever attacks/deffends changes after 12 rounds. In order to win rounds the attackers have to either kill the entire enemy team or plant a spike that detonates after 40 seconds. As a deffender you have to either kill the entire enemy team or defuse the spike when it has been planted to win.
